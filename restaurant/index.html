We would like to start by welcoming you to our family-owned pizzeria that was established in 2015. My wife and I have always enjoyed hosting parties and making food for our family and friends in our home in Hanover, MN. Our guests continually raved about the pizzas we made and insisted we open a restaurant. With the help and input of our family and friends we developed a menu full of our most delicious pizza combinations. Our name was inspired by my best friend, Mario, when he took his first bite of our peppy pepperoni pizza and said, "Now that's a pizza!" We are extremely happy and blessed to be able to share our passion for pizza and hope that our restaurant continues to bring family and friends together for years-to-come.
